body: >-


In this 3 day training we will use non-formal education methods to challenge and unlearn our internalisation of human-nature dualism, human supremacy and separation. We will relearn how to feel and act as part of nature. Sessions include: mapping systems of oppression; forest walks; input from experts, exercises drawn from Gesturing Towards Decolonial Futures (GTDF), and more! When? Friday 20 - Sunday 22 February, with the first session on Friday afternoon at 16h, and we'll wrap things up by 17h on Sunday afternoon Where? Can Masdeu, Barcelona (L3 Canyelles), Catalunya This training is free of charge, thanks to a microgrant from the International Degrowth Network (IDN), this includes meals and accommodation at Can Masdeu.  The training will be in English, please let us know in the application form if you have any translation needs, and we'll try to accommodate.
